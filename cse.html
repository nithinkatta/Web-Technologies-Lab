Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an academic program that involves both scientific and engineering aspects of computing. It is a four-year undergraduate engineering course that covers a variety of topics related to computation, such as: Analysis of algorithms, Programming languages, Program design, Software, Computer hardware. CSE graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills to design, develop, implement, and maintain computer systems, software applications, and networks. CSE has roots in electrical engineering, mathematics, and linguistics. CSE helps fields such as electrical and electronics engineering, information technology, and software engineering.
