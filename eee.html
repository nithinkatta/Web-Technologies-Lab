EEE stands for Electrical and Electronics Engineering. It is a four-year undergraduate course in India that covers the study of electrical engineering and electronics research. The course covers the following topics: Power systems, Signal processing, Programming, Circuits and systems, Control engineering, Microprocessors, Electrical machines, Circuit analysis, Electromagnetism, Instruments. EEE engineers focus on the following: Analysis, Design, Development, Manufacture. Some of the fields within EEE include: Microelectronics Deals with small electronic components, such as transistors and integrated circuits Electronics and Communication Engineering Deals with the design, development, and implementation of electronic devices, circuits, and communication systems Electrical engineers specialize in the design and development of electricity-related products, such as power systems and household appliances. They work with electronics and transmission and distribution engineering fields to create essential components for modern living.
