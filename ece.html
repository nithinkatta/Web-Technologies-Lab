Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) is an engineering branch that involves the research, design, development, and testing of electronic equipment. ECE engineers also conceptualize and oversee the manufacturing of communications and broadcast systems. ECE involves research, design, development, and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems. This includes: Power and control systems Artificial intelligence Machine learning Smart devices Electronic circuits Communication devices like transmitters, receivers, and integrated circuits ECE also includes the production, design, research, and testing of electronic devices and technology issues related to telecommunications systems, electronics, computers, and related industrial sectors.
